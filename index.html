About afyaboratz

afyaboratz is a starter landing page template. It's intended for quick demos, MVPs, and personal project pages. No frameworks required — just plain HTML and CSS.

  • Accessible markup
  • Semantic structure
  • Simple forms and CTAs

Quick start

  1. Customize text & colors in styles.css
  2. Add your logo at logo.svg
  3. Open index.html in a browser
